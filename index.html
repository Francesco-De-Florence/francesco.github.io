About Me

I am currently pursuing my Master of Science (Engg.) in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the Islamic University, Bangladesh. My research focuses on leveraging Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) to optimize wireless communication networks and smart grid systems. [cite: 4, 14]


As a Research Assistant at the AI & ML Lab, I am developing deep learning frameworks for real-time physical violence detection using Wi-Fi CSI signals. My long-term goal is to contribute to the development of robust 6G communication and intelligent energy management systems. [cite: 22, 14]

Research Interests

  • Deep Learning for 5G/6G (OFDM & PAPR)
  • Wi-Fi Sensing & Human Activity Recognition
  • Machine Learning in Smart Grid Security
  • Explainable AI (XAI) for Wireless Networks
  • Visible Light Communication (VLC)
